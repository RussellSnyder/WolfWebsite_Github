The Frequency

The Frequency is a Finnish-German band, with three albums that feature an eclectic mix of styles, from ambient to folk to dub to electronic - and more.

Their music can be found here.

The Frequency are: Minna Aalto, Michl Britsch, Wolfgang Thomas
