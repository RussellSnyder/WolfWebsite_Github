Die brotlose Brotdose

Die brotlose Brotdose is a live electronic improvisational trio from Frankfurt, Germany.

If you are easily offended by limbo, lo-fi, inertia, and only partly controlled chaos, do not listen to this.

Die brotlose Brotdose are: Frank Bossert, Chris Rücker, Wolfgang Thomas
