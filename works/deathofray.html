2045 - The Death Of Ray Kurzweil:

alternative sound design

This is an alternative sound design for an excerpt of a film by Fabien Giraud and Raphael Siboni. (To see the original film, and other highly captivating works, please go to www.theunmanned.com). It is an abstract sonic narrative, based on my fragmented impression of what the title of the film, the world it depicts, and its possible connotations might suggest. In this, it represents my subjective interpretation of the visual material, and its context - in effect, a re-interpretation, and re-contextualisation of the original piece of art.

It should be noted that my work was not commissioned in any way by Fabien Giraud and Raphael Siboni, whom I greatly thank for giving me permission to use their work.
