Operation Cue

Operation Cue is an improvisational ensemble sound performance to an excerpt of the film by the same name, depicting a US nuclear bomb test in 1955. The performers use self-designed Max/MSP patches and different controllers to accompany in real time the visual material. The recording presented here was captured after minimal rehearsal time, and is fully improvised.

Performers: Ross Taylor, Wolfgang Thomas, Richard West
