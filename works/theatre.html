Works for Theatre and Performance

I have done sound work for theatre and performance for many years, and continue to do so.

I have extensive professional experience in this field, and have worked as music editor, composer, musician, and sound designer for productions at renowned theatres, among them Theater Basel, Schauspiel Düsseldorf, Schauspiel Frankfurt, and more.
