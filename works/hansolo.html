Solo/Miscellaneous Music

My work in music extends over a wide range of activities, and I am grateful to have had the opportunity to perform with some extraordinarily talented and accomplished musicians over the years, especially with Cameron Macnair.

I also compose, record, and produce solo work from time to time, some of which is publicly available, some of which is not, some of which rots away in a corner somewhere.
