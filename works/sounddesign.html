Miscellaneous Sound Design Work

Having initially worked in sound design only on a slightly irregular basis, mostly for theatre and performance (excluding the "sound design" work that comes with being a partly "electronic" musician), over time I found myself moving in this direction more and more, to the point where I committed myself to a Masters' programme, from which I graduated with distinction in 2014.

I have worked within different contexts, from live Foley to field recordings, from creating sound and impulse response libraries to creating sound designs for moving images and interactive environments.
