The Tuned City was originally conceived as part of my Masters' Thesis for the MSc in Sound Design at The University of Edinburgh, entitled "The Tuned City - A performative approach to exploring sound in liminal urban spaces" It consists of a series of performances in publicly accessible urban environments, focussing on the inherent liminality of the specific locations. In these performances, neurofeedback-controlled digital instruments are employed, generating pitched sounds from the sonic environment, which are then re-inserted into it.

As all recordings are binaural, listening on headphones is strongly recommended.
