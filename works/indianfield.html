Indian Field Recordings

During a one month stay in Gujarat, India, working on "Second Hand Cities" I collected a substantial amount of field recordings with a handheld device.

Some of these have evolved into short pieces of music, such as "Hasumati", which uses two very different recordings as base material for a sound miniature.
